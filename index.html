What we do

Emoti is an edtech product aimed at grades 5-8 that uses virtual reality to teach emotional self-recognition and regulation in order to create a personalized Social Emotional Learning (SEL) educational experience for all. Psychology studies have shown time and again the positive impact of emotional intelligence on personal, professional, and academic futures.

How does Emoti work? We teach SEL through showing students VR simulations and measuring emotional response to these VR simulations using wearable technology, where we're able to measure physiological signs like blood pressure, skin conductance, etc. and map them to emotional states. Therefore, we're able to quantitatively track how students perform within the simulation, and can change the simulation as necessary to elicit the appropriate emotional response.
